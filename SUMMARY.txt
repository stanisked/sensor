🚀 QUICK START:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. Build:
     cd ~/cobot_ws
     colcon build --symlink-install

  2. Source:
     source install/setup.bash

  3. Run:
     ros2 launch cobot_bringup robot.launch.py

  4. Control (in another terminal):
     source install/setup.bash
     ros2 topic pub /cmd_vel geometry_msgs/Twist 'linear: {x: 0.5}'
